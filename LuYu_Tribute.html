Lu Yu - Tea Sage of China

Painting of Lu Yu drinking tea.

    A Rough Timeline of Lu Yu's Life

  • 733 C.E. - Born in Tianmen, Hubei province.
  • 746 - Studied with Zou Fuzi at Huomen mountain. During this time, he became known as a knowledgable herbalist as he cared for his master and fellow students. During this time, Lu Yu's obsession with tea, and by proxy water, led Zou Fuzi to dig a well around Lu Yu's favorite spring. One thousand years later, this well saved Jingling from drought.
  • 752 - Concluded studies at Huomen mountain and returned to Jingling. Here, Lu Yu met and became friends wiht Cui Guofu, a government official as well as scholar and poet. Together, the two traveled, drank tea, and wrote poems. Under Cui Guofu's tutelage, Lu Yu's writing matured. During their travels, Lu Yu wrote The Classic of Tea, a set of three books used and revered to this day for its transmittence of the scholar's knowledge.
  • 804 - Lu Yu dies at the age of 73.
